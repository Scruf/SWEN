About

HealthNet is a new and efficient way for doctors, nurses, and patients to interact with each other.Whether you're a patient who is trying to set an appointment with a doctor or trying to access his/her medical information, a doctor who wants to communicate with patient and/or create and update medical information, or a nurse who can communicate with doctors and patients to help modify medical information and appointments, this Software is the perfect starting point for an efficient future!
